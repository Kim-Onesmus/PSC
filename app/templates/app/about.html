About Us

About Us

Proposal Specialists Consultancy (PSC) is a registered professional fundraising and resource mobilization consultancy firm in Kenya dedicated to helping organizations access the financial resources they need to thrive. We support institutions in crafting compelling grant proposals, fundraising strategies, and donor engagement plans that secure sustainable funding.

Beyond writing, we offer tailored solutions in resource mobilization, business planning, project management, and donor/partner mapping for self-help groups, community-based organizations (CBOs), non-governmental organizations (NGOs), government entities, faith-based organizations, schools, hospitals, and entrepreneurs.

Our ultimate goal is to unlock resources and opportunities for our clients through well-structured fundraising strategies and impactful proposals that drive lasting social and economic transformation.

Vision

To be the leading fundraising and resource mobilization consultancy in Africa, empowering organizations to access sustainable funding and create lasting development impact.

Mission

To provide high-quality, customized resource mobilization, grant writing, and donor engagement services that strengthen organizations’ ability to secure funding, diversify revenue streams, and successfully implement their projects.
